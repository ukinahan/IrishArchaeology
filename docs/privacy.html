Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026 · Evin Cairn — Irish Archaeology · v1

Evin Cairn ("the app") is a field guide to Ireland's archaeological monuments. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in accordance with the EU GDPR and Irish data-protection law.

What we collect

With your explicit consent, we collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics — which screens you visit and which features you use (e.g. "opened map", "saved a site"). No personal identifiers are attached.
  • Crash & error reports — automatic stack traces if the app misbehaves, so we can diagnose and fix bugs.

You can opt in or out at any time from Settings → Privacy in the app.

What we do not collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • Your precise location. The app uses your location on the device only to show what's near you and to plan trips. Location data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Photos, files, calendar entries, or any other personal content from your device.
  • Advertising identifiers (we don't show ads).

Third-party services

  • National Monuments Service (Ireland) — public ArcGIS service queried for archaeological site records. No personal data is sent.
  • Mapbox — basemap tiles. Mapbox may log anonymised tile requests per their privacy policy.
  • Wikimedia / Wikipedia — site photos. Public API; no personal data sent.
  • Sentry (only if you accept crash reports) — receives error stack traces.
  • PostHog (only if you accept analytics) — receives anonymous event names.

Data retention

Anonymous analytics events are retained for 12 months and then deleted. Crash reports are retained for 90 days. You can request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us (see below).

Your rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident you have the right to access, rectify, or erase the data we process about you, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights or for any question, contact us at privacy@irisharchaeology.app.

Children

Evin Cairn is suitable for general audiences but is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes we will prompt you to review this policy in the app before continuing.
