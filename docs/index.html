Privacy Policy — Evin Cairn

Last updated: 22 April 2026
Developer: Ultan Kinahan (Individual)
Contact: ultanki@outlook.com

Evin Cairn ("the app") is a free field guide to publicly recorded archaeological monuments in the Republic of Ireland. We have designed it to collect as little information as possible.

Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers or any contact details
  • User accounts (the app has no sign-in)
  • Analytics, advertising, or tracking identifiers
  • Crash reports tied to your identity
  • Browsing history, search history, or in-app activity
  • Photos, contacts, calendar, microphone or camera data

We do not use any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Facebook SDK, etc.), or behavioural-tracking tools.

Location

If you grant location permission, the app uses your device's location only on your device to:

  1. Show archaeological sites near you on the map.
  2. Power the "What Am I Looking At?" inference feature.
  3. Send a coarse bounding box (a rectangle of latitude/longitude) to the public National Monuments Service (NMS) ArcGIS API so it can return the sites in that area.

Your precise location is never transmitted to any server operated by the developer. The NMS API receives only the rough viewport you are looking at, not a personal identifier.

You can revoke location permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → Evin Cairn.

Data stored on your device

For offline use, the app caches site records you have already loaded (using Expo AsyncStorage). This cache is stored only on your device and never transmitted. You can clear it by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Sites you tap "Save" are stored locally only.

Third-party services

The app fetches data from, and renders content provided by, the following third parties. Their own privacy policies apply to data they receive:

  • National Monuments Service (Ireland) ArcGIS Feature Service — to retrieve site records (CC BY 4.0). archaeology.ie
  • Mapbox — to render the map and tiles. Mapbox may receive standard request metadata (IP address, device type) needed to deliver map tiles. mapbox.com/legal/privacy
  • Apple App Store / TestFlight — for distribution and crash reporting. Apple's privacy policy applies to that data. apple.com/legal/privacy

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your rights (GDPR)

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, export or delete. If you have any questions, you can contact us at the email address above.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, mention the change in the next app update.
